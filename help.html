When is Refreshers?

Your freshers’ fortnight runs from Monday 27 September until Friday 8 September. We’ll be hosting a variety of daytime and evening events every weekday.

Who can come to events?

All new and continuing undergraduate and postgraduate students at Central are welcome at Refreshers. Some events will have limited tickets available on the door for guests accompanied by a Central student.

Will I need to bring ID?

Some events will require proof of age either at the door or when buying drinks. You’ll also need your Central campus card for some events.

How do I get home after a late night?

We recommend downloading the free Citymapper app to help you plan your way around London. Last trains are usually just after midnight, but many bus services run 24 hours a day. Try not to travel alone late at night and speak to us if you need help or find yourself on your own.

Uber, Bolt and Free Now are examples of minicab booking apps. They are often much cheaper than black cabs, especially with friends.

Never use an unbooked minicab – they are dangerous and illegal.

What if I need help?

Our team of officers will be available at every event. They’re happy to help if anything goes wrong. In an emergency, dial 999.

How will you keep me safe from coronavirus?

We’re asking everyone coming to an evening event to take a lateral flow test beforehand. Some events are also available to join from home, if you’d rather not come on site, and we’re working with venues to keep you safe. Central have also been kept up to date with our planning.

Do I need a ticket?

We know moving to university can be expensive, so we’ll be running as many free events as possible. Events hosted in clubs and bars will have door charges, but you can get entry to all events by buying a Refreshers wristband. A wristband will save you money compared to the door charges. Some events require a wristband, as we can’t offer tickets on the door.

How do I get a wristband?

Nice choice. You can order your Refreshers wristband via this website. Visit the tickets page, choose the best package for you and complete payment via PayPal. You don’t need an account, as debit and credit cards are also accepted.

Can I buy a wristband for someone else?

Of course! The same applies to someone buying a wristband for you. Just visit the tickets page, choose a package and complete payment via PayPal. Make sure you use the name and student number of the person you’re buying for, so we know it’s for them.

Are finale tickets available to pre-order?

Freshers’ finale tickets will be available to pre-order soon. Check back for more information.

Where do I collect my wristband?

You’ll be able to collect your wristband from the first few events. Make sure you bring your Central campus card, so we know it’s you.
